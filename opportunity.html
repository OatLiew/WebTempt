The market opportunity

By 2014, the U.S. HVAC equipment market is projected to be $39 billion and the filtration market $3.3 billion, with 3% annual growth. This growth will be fueled by replacement of aging HVAC equipment, increased demand for energy efficient equipment, and government regulation of indoor air quality (IAQ). Commercial HVAC filtration comprises $1.6 billion of the total filtration market, and 80% of the commercial market is serviced with MERV-rated filters (as opposed to HEPA, etc). Thus, the addressable US commercial filtration market is approximately $1.3 billion. Figure 1 provides a snapshot of key statistics.
